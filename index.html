3.Kunye Festival

KUNYE is a vibrant South African music and cultural festival that brings together Afro house, deep house and electronic sounds in a celebration of unity and community. Launched by DJ Shimza, it showcases both local and international talent while highlighting the rich creativity of African music. The festival's name means “together as one” and it's immersive stages and energetic crowd embody that spirit. Whether at beachside venues or outdoor stages, KUNYE offers a soulful, rhythm driven experience that connects people through music.

4.Cape Town International Jazz Festival

The Cape Town International Jazz Festival is one of Africa's most prestigious music events, bringing together world class jazz, soul, funk, and fusion artists from around the globe. Held annually at the Cape Town International Convention Centre, it blends rich musical heritage with fresh innovation in a vibrant, multi stage atmosphere. Known as “Africa's Grandest Gathering,” the festival celebrates both established legends and emerging talent against the stunning backdrop of the Mother City. It's a must attend experience for lovers of live music and culture
